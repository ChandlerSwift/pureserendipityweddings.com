Where do we start?

  1. Contact us! Shoot us an email or fill out the contact sheet.
  2. Meet up! We’ll set up a meeting that makes sense—in person, on the phone, over Zoom, whatever fits! Tell us about your story. Then, we’ll work out a package or a customized plan to best fit your vision.
  3. Make it official! Once we have a plan, you’ll sign on the dotted line, pay a deposit, and celebrate. We’ll have your back for your big day!
  4. Plan, plan, plan! We’ll help you find your vendors, work out a beautiful design, and keep everything going smoothly.
  5. Get married!! We’ll keep everything on track and YOU will have the best day ever!
