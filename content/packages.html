Full Planning Package

Packages starting at $5,350

This package is meant to let you relax and enjoy yourself all the way from engagement to your wedding day! We will research and recommend all of your vendors, help you choose a color palette, and manage the whole planning and day of process to make sure you can relax and take in the experience from start to finish!

Partial Planning Package

Packages starting at $2,650

This is a great fit for the couple who wants to be hands on with planning, but doesn't want to handle all the logistics. We will help you create a planning timeline, review contracts, help choose decor, manage vendor communications, and assist with all of the day-of details! With partial planning, we will ensure all your details are ready to go so you can just sit back and enjoy your day!
